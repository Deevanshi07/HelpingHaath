People for Animals (PFA) is a passionate college society committed to the welfare of animals on our campus, including stray dogs and birds. We work to provide these animals with food, medical care, and shelter, ensuring their safety and well-being within our campus community. Through regular feeding, vaccinations, and health checks, we aim to give them a better quality of life. However, our work relies on the support of generous donors. By contributing, you can help us continue to care for and protect these animals. Join us in making a difference!
